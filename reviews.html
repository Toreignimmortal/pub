ULTAR – KADATH

"This is Lovecraft with a Siberian edge – a wonderful journey over violent crests and through bursting snowstorms of melodic beauty. Kadath is merely Ultar’s debut record, but it stands for quality post-black metal."



FRIEDA'S STILL IN LOVE – DIEZ INVIERNOS

"Never before did music taste so much like a fata morgana of the place it came from. Diez Inviernos is an album that resonates estranged romanticism – ten long winters of looking out of a window, with eyes that go out of focus and limbs heavy as lead."



VEKTOR – TERMINAL REDUX

"It’s all coming back to me now. Not Celine Dion, but that intense rush of adrenaline and power that is felt within the riffing and grooving of speed metal bands. [...] Thrash metal can still be relevant these days."



ORSON HENTSCHEL – FEED THE TAPE

"Rarely have I been touched more by an experimental record. [...] Feed the Tape is a veritable addition to the landscape of avant-garde art and sees Orson Hentschel at the front of a movement towards the future of music."



THE PINEAPPLE THIEF – YOUR WILDERNESS

"Giving credit where credit is due, The Pineapple Thief have absolutely done a fine job with this record. [...] Your Wilderness is an intriguing, well-written record that might be enjoyed casually, but it does the album a disservice to not regard it within the scope of its theme."
